History

Odsessy was released in 2021 as our second major expansion.

It brings atmospheric landings and on-foot activities.

New player on-foot equipment and new on-foot concourses

Atmospheric Landings

The landmark feature of Odyssey is the addition of the ability to explore planets on foot.

Odyssey allows planets with tenuous or thin atmospheres to be landed on.

Players can now explore and fly on these new planets.

Space Legs

In a first for Elite this update allows the player to take direct control of their in-game avatar and explore the game without needing a ship or vehicle.

With the new equipment you can explore whole planets and settlements.

With a new role Exobiology focuses on identifying and cataloguing alien flora found on planetary surfaces using the Genetic Sampler tool.

Scorpion

A new military SRV, with increased firepower, armour and shields compared to the Scarab.

Ground Combat

This Mercenary role involves working as a gun for hire.

Show off none flying skills in this new role.

Mercenaries can also participate in large-scale battles for control of settlements.
