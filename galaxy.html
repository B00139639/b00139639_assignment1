Welcome to the Elite Dangerous Galaxy

Elite Dangerous is a space simulation game based in a 1 to 1 scale model of the Milky Way Galaxy. Using our Stellar Forge technology, we have create an expansive galaxy with different entities.

Gravity

With 400 Billion star-systems to explore. Many have planets with gravity and tidal forces modeled.

Star Systems

With true scientfic classifaction, we have modeled Main Sequence Stars, Giants and Supergiants, Proto-Stars, Neutron Stars, White Dwarfs and Black Holes in amazing detail and quality.

Planets

We have modeled Terrestrial Planets, Gas Giants, Moons and Planetary Rings. Many are explorable and landable.
