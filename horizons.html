History

Horizons was released in 2016, our first major update.

It was continuously updated between 2016 and 2017.

This added much more gameplay to the Elite universe.

Engineers

As part of this update we released the Titular Engineers

Engineers are 30 NPCs (Non Player Characters) scattered across human space in remote planetary outposts who can upgrade the weapons and modules of player ships.

In addition, the standard mission UI (User Interface) was improved.

SRVs

Let us introduce you to the SRV. These SRVs (Surface Recon Vehicles) 'Scarab' can be used to traverse the surface of planets and moons.

The Scarab is capable of impressive speeds, 38 meters per second on flat terrain.

Defend yourself with a rapid fire, independantly controlled turret.

4 tonnes of cargo space allows you to collect resources from any planet you land on.

Planetside

Land on planets in our first expansion, with new coding, the landings are graphically seamless with only a small pause for instance loading. Fly in the gravity of planets with the new orbital flight model.

Land at new surface settlements and surface ports.

Explore ship wrecks, geologic sites and biologic sites.
